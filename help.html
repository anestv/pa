Help - Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?

A website where you can ask questions to other users and answer their answers. You can control who can ask questions to you and who can see your answers.

How can I search for questions or users?

Go to the Search page

Do I need to create an account to ask questions?

It depends on who you want to ask. Some users let everyone ask them, whereas some require an account.

Do I need an account to answer?

Yes. How would others be able to ask you if you didn't have one?

Who can view questions I have answered?

You can manage this in your settings. By default only your friends can.

Who can ask me a question?

You can manage this in your settings. By default only your friends can.

What should I do if a question or answer is annoying me?

If a question asked to you is annoying you, delete it. Otherwise, report it to us by clicking the 'Report' link.

How can I change my account info, friends and password?

Go to Settings.

How can I delete my account?

Go to Settings and click 'Delete my account'.

What about copyright?

All rights are reserved by each question/answer's author. When asking or answering make sure that you are the owner of what you upload. See the Terms for more.

Who made this website?

As of now, Anestis Varsamidis. His sister and father also helped him with some issues.

Can I see the source code? Can I help you on an issue?

Sure! Just go to our GitHub page!

Where can I report a bug or suggest a new feature?

At our issues page on GitHub.

What if I have a question that's not in this list?

Ask it at the PrivateAsk profile!
